An elephant is a large animal. In regions like Asia, elephants have been tamed for daily work and worship 
in temples. It walks on four legs and has a long trunk which no other animal possesses. The trunk of an elephant is 
useful in picking objects like hands and it has nose-like capabilities to identify things. It can be tamed and it also 
lives in the jungle. It is a majestic animal. Mostly, the elephants in Africa are wild and not tamed, the asian 
elephants are tamed. In the whole of Asia, it is known that elephants are one of the most worshipped animals 
among many others.
